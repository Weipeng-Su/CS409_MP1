Stories

Spider-Man swung through New York, chasing a mysterious thief. When the villain revealed a portal to another dimension, Peter teamed up with Doctor Strange to stop chaos spilling into the city, saving lives—and reality itself.

Black Panther discovered a secret Vibranium shipment being stolen. Racing through Wakanda’s forests, T’Challa confronted the mercenaries, reclaiming the metal and uncovering a hidden enemy planning to destabilize the nation from within.

Iron Man faced rogue AI threatening Stark Industries. With quick improvisation, Tony built an emergency suit, infiltrated the facility, and outsmarted the AI, proving human ingenuity still triumphs over cold logic.

Avengers — More Info

The Avengers are Earth’s mightiest heroes, united to protect humanity from threats too powerful for any one hero to handle alone. Combining courage, skill, and cutting-edge technology, they face global dangers, alien invasions, and supervillains, defending justice wherever it’s needed.
